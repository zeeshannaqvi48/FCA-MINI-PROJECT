Introduction

Self-awareness among Gen Z survey helps us to examine how they define and perceive self-awareness and how it relates to their daily lives. The survey seeks to identify factors that include social media, family, friends, education, and to investigate the relationship between self-awareness and mental health. In an effort to better understand the habits and attitudes, we conducted a google survey of respondents aged 17-30. The survey aimed to explore the ways in which Gen Z expresses themselves, the factors that influence self-expression, and the role of social media in shaping their identities.

OBJECTIVE
The goal of this report is to gain a deeper understanding of self- expression among Gen Z, examining various forms and the factors that influence it. Using a google survey, we collected data from a sample of Gen Z individuals and analyzed it using excel, employing formulas, graphs and statistical techniques to identify trends and patterns. We also utilized set theory concepts such as union, intersection, and venn diagrams, to visualize the relationships between different variables and identify areas of overlap and divergence. By leveraging these analytical methods, this report provides insights into the self-expression habits and attitudes of Gen Z, and offers recommendations for educators, marketers, and mental health professionals seeking to support and promote self-expression among this generation.

Analysis

Self-Expression Channels:


The survey revealed that college students primarily express themselves through social media, followed by in-person interactions and creative outlets such as art and music.

Factors Influencing Self-Expression:


The survey identified social media, societal movements, and college environments as key factors influencing self-expression among college students.

Impact of Social Media:


Social media platforms such as Instagram, Snapchat, and TikTok play a significant role in shaping how college students express themselves, with many respondents citing the pressure to present an idealized version of themselves online.

Role of College:


College environments provide students with opportunities to explore and express their identities, often leading to increased openness and authenticity.

Future of Self-Expression:


Respondents expressed optimism about the future of self-expression, with many believing that Gen Z will continue to embrace individuality and authenticity in their self-expression.

Key Insights and Inference

College as a Catalyst for Self-Expression:


The college environment significantly influences students' self-expression, often leading to increased openness and authenticity.
The Impact of Technology and Society:
Social media and societal movements play a crucial role in shaping students' self-expression, both positively and negatively.

Key Insights


College students value self- expression and often become more open in expressing themselves during their college years. Social media and societal movements significantly influence how college students express themselves.

Recommendation

safe spaces for diverse self-expression.

Educate students on responsible social media use.

Conclusion

The survey highlights the significant role of college in shaping students' self-expression. By focusing on inclusive environments, promoting mental health, and encouraging digital literacy, institutions can empower students to embrace their individuality and contribute positively to society.
